Introduction

About Me

I'm a cybersecurity research engineer with a passion for discovering vulnerabilities and building robust security solutions. My work spans across vulnerability research, reverse engineering, and security automation.

With expertise in both offensive and defensive security, I focus on understanding attack vectors to build better defenses. I've contributed to securing critical infrastructure, discovered high-impact vulnerabilities, and developed tools that help security teams work more efficiently.

My research interests include binary exploitation, fuzzing techniques, malware analysis, and cryptographic implementations. I believe in responsible disclosure and actively contribute to the security community through publications, talks, and open-source projects.
